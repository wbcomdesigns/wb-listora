## Business Claims

Business claims allow real business owners to claim ownership of their listing, giving them the ability to edit and manage it.

### How Claims Work

1. A visitor finds an unclaimed listing
2. They click "Claim this listing" on the detail page
3. They fill in their details and provide verification
4. An admin reviews the claim
5. If approved, the claimant becomes the listing owner

### Claim Verification

When submitting a claim, users provide:

- **Business role:** Owner, Manager, or Authorized Representative
- **Verification notes:** How they can prove ownership
- **Contact information:** Phone number or email for verification

### Managing Claims

Go to **Listora > Claims** to review pending claims:

- View claim details and verification information
- Approve claims to transfer ownership
- Reject claims with an optional reason
- Filter by status (Pending, Approved, Rejected)

### What Happens on Approval

When a claim is approved:

1. The listing's author is changed to the claiming user
2. The user can now edit the listing from their dashboard
3. A verification badge appears on the listing
4. The user receives an email notification

### Settings

Under **Listora > Settings > Claims:**

- **Enable claims:** Toggle the claim system on/off
- **Auto-approve:** Automatically approve claims (not recommended)
- **Require login:** Only logged-in users can submit claims
